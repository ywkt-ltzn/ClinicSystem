    About Us

    Pun Hlaing Hospitals in Yangon is our flagship for nationwide healthcare. It is the only hospital in Myanmar, which is accredited by Joint Commission International, USA. Pun Hlaing Hospitals was founded by Serge Pun @ U Theim Wai in 2005 and is managed by FMI of Yoma Group in partnership with OUE Healthcare Singapore.

    Our vision is to spearhead international quality healthcare for the nation, and to reach widely through telehealth, own and partner facilities, home care, as well as free mobile clinics and dedicated beds for those, who cannot afford the much needed family and specialist care.

    Yoma-OUE Healthcare is committed to bring the care into health led by our flagship healthcare provider Pun Hlaing Hospitals, their staff and pool of full time dedicated doctors. The first Pun Hlaing hospital was established by Serge Pun @ U Theim Wai in Yangon in 2005. It is the only hospital in Myanmar to be awarded the American Joint Commission International Accreditation for quality and patient safety.

    International Patient Centre

    Pun Hlaing International Patient Centre is a one-stop service for international patients looking for specialist expertise and personalised patient care with state-of-the- art equipment at Pun Hlaing Hospitals. Our dedicated and well-trained coordinators provide a seamless insurance payment process and attend the special needs of patients before, during, and after the visit.
